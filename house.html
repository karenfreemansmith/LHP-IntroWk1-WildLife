The Money Pit

The house sat empty for about 7 years before my boyfriend bought it. It is constantly under construction as he tears up the walls to fix the wiring. It needs a new roof and new carpet (especially where the roof leaked!).

Each new project unearths archeological finds like mummified mice, old newspapers, children's toys, and scary wallpaper from the 60s.
